What is this year's special theme?

Sundown With Love

“Not all heroes wear capes.”
A part of Sundown Marathon’s Charity outreach campaign.

Every year, Sundown with Love aims to gather the running community for one simple reason – to give back to the community. This year, OSIM Sundown Marathon Singapore aims to champion the cause of inclusivity in sports and the wider community and will lead by example to contribute funds to the ongoing charity initiative – Sundown with Love. The beneficiaries are Singapore Disability Sports Council and The Straits Times School Pocket Money Fund. For every race slot purchased, $1 of the registration fee will be donated to the two beneficiaries, and runners will also have the opportunity to donate additional funds to the causes via the website.

Singapore Disability Sports Council

A national sports body
an advocate of disability sports for the past 45 years.

The goal of SDSC is to enable PWD to realize their potential through sports. Founded on the belief in the dignity, capability and value of persons with disabilities (PWD), their mission is to enable our sportspersons with disabilities to realise their individual potential by providing them with the opportunity to participate and excel in sports both recreationally and competitively.

Staits Times School Pocket Money Fund

A community project initiated
to provide financial aid to children from low-income families.

Since the project inception in 2000, the Fund has helped over 160,000 cases of children and youth in need and disbursed over $60m. They can use this money for school-related expenses, such as buying a meal during recess, paying for transport or use it to meet other schooling needs. The financial help also eases the burden of the many parents who are already struggling to feed their families on their meagre income.
